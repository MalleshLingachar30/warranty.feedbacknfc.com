Frequently Asked Questions

How does this differ from traditional warranty management software?

Traditional warranty tools require training, manual entry, and phone calls just to start a service request. FeedbackNFC replaces that with a tap-first flow where the smart sticker opens the right experience instantly, technician assignment is automated, and documentation is captured as the work happens.

Do customers need to download an app?

No! That's the beauty of NFC technology. When customers tap the sticker with their smartphone, it opens directly in their mobile browser. No app download, no account creation, no barriers. Works with any NFC-enabled phone (95%+ of smartphones in Saudi Arabia).

What if a customer doesn't have an NFC-enabled phone?

Every NFC sticker also includes a QR code as backup. Customers can scan the code with their camera app and get the same experience, giving you full smartphone compatibility without changing the workflow.

How does warranty activation work?

Warranty can start right at the point of sale using the carton sticker marked "Activate Warranty Now." The product sticker remains on the unit and is marked "Scan for Warranty Service" for future support. This gives you a clean separation between activation and after-sales service access.

Can this integrate with our existing systems?

Yes! FeedbackNFC has API endpoints that can integrate with existing ERP, CMMS, and warranty management systems. We can also work as a standalone platform if you prefer. During the pilot, we'll assess your current systems and recommend the best integration approach.

What's the pricing after the 3-month free pilot?

Pricing is based on the number of devices under warranty: $2-6 per device per year, depending on volume. For a manufacturer selling 1,000 devices/year with 2-year warranties (2,000 devices under warranty at any time), the annual cost would be $6,000-12,000. Given that you'll save $60,000+ annually, the ROI is 500-1000%. We'll provide exact pricing based on your volume during the pilot discussions.

Is this compliant with Saudi warranty regulations?

Yes. FeedbackNFC captures warranty activation timestamps, proof of purchase date, service history, and customer confirmation to support Saudi warranty compliance and digital audit requirements.

Does it work in Arabic?

Yes. Customer-facing pages, notifications, and warranty certificates support both English and Arabic, and the Saudi landing page keeps a dedicated Arabic toggle.

What happens if we're not satisfied after the pilot?

Simple: you walk away, no payment owed. The 3-month pilot is completely free with no strings attached.
